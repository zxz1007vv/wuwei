Participants

The 32 participants of the main tournament are:

  • 11 from Japan: Cho U, Hane Naoki, Hikosaka Naoto, Imamura Toshiya, Kim Sujun, Kono Rin, Ogata Masaki, Takao Shinji, Yamada Kimio, Yamashita Keigo, Yoda Norimoto

  • 8 from Korea: Cho Hanseung, Han Sanghun, Hong Seongji, Lee Changho, Lee Sedol, Mok Jinseok, Park Jungsang, Park Yeonghun
  • 1 from Taiwan: Zhou Junxun
  • 3 from Europe: Alexander Dinerchtein (Russia), Cristian Pop (Germany), Ilya Shikshin (Russia)
  • 2 from North America: Jiang Mingjiu, Li Jie
  • 1 from South America: Fernando Aguilar (Argentina)
  • 1 from the rest of the world: Yang Shihai (Hong Kong)
