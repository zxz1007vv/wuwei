All Japan Hayago Championship

The All Japan Hayago Championship (全日本早碁選手権大會), or Nikkan Sports Outdoor Hayago Championship, was a once-only event held in 1949, won by Miyashita Shuyo.

All Japan First Place Title

The All Japan 1st Place Tournament (全日本第一位決定戦) was the successor of the Nihon-Kiin 1st Place Tournament (which had been open to Nihon Ki-in players only). It was held 5 times, from 1970 to 1975, and won all five times by Otake Hideo.
