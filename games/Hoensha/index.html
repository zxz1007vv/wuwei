Hoensha start

The Hoensha (方円社; an organization of professional go players) started in 1879. Records of the games played at their monthly meetings were published in Igo Shinpo (囲棋新報). Below the 53 games from the first volume of Igo Shinpo. The games are given in publication order, which may differ from chronological order. Handicap is indicated by 'H'.
