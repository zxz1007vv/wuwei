Aizu Cup

The Aizu Chuo Hospital Cup (会津中央病院杯) or Women's Tachiaoi Cup (女流立葵杯) is a go tournament for female pros, first held in 2014.

Time is 1h each game, but for the 2-day final 5h (1st and 2nd editions), 6h (3rd edition). Komi is 6.5.
