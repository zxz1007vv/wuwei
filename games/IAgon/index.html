Japan-China Agon-Kiriyama Match

Each year, since 2000, following the Chinese and Japanese Agon-Kiriyama Cup tournaments, the two winners play a single game alternatingly in China and Japan. We shall call it the Japan-China Agon-Kiriyama Match (阿含桐山杯日中決戦).

The rules of the host country are followed. In particular, komi is 6.5 points in Japan, and 3.75 zi in China (since the fifth edition; the first four years komi was 5.5 / 2.75 zi).
