Pro-Ama Mixed Tournament 2019

Tournament between 32 players (8 male pros, 8 female pros, 8 male amas, 8 female amas).

In the 1st round these four groups were paired internally. In the 2nd round four male pros played against four male amas, and four female pros against four female amas. In all cases the pro won, except for Shibano Toramaru who was beaten by Kurita Yoshiki. The tournament was won by Ko Iso.

Komi was 6.5 within each group, 2.5 for female pro against male pro, −2.5 for female ama against female pro, and −6.5 for male ama against male pro.

Time was 20 min with 30 sec byoyomi, and 30 min for the final.
