1st Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Ida Atsushi (8p), Ichiriki Ryo (7p), Sun Zhe (3p), Tsuruta Kazushi (2p), Kyo Kagen (2p), and Koyama Kuya (1p).
Korea: Na Hyun (4p), Choi Jeong (4p), Shin Jinseo (2p).
China: Lian Xiao (7p), Xia Chenkun (3p), Li Qincheng (1p).
Taiwan: Lin Junyan (6p).
Europe: Lukas Podpera (6d ama).
America: Gansheng Shi (1p).
Asia/Oceania: Joanne Missingham (Hei Jiajia, 6p).
