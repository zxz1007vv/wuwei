3rd Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Ichiriki Ryo (7p), Sun Zhe (4p), Kyo Kagen (3p), Mutsuura Yuta (2p), Shibano Toramaru (2p), and Onishi Ryuhei (1p).
Korea: Lee Donghun (5p), Shin Jinseo (5p), and Byun Sangil (4p).
China: Fan Yunruo (4p), Yang Dingxin (3p), and Li Qincheng (1p).
Taiwan: Lin Shixun (5p).
Europe: Grigorii Fionin (7d ama).
America: Justin Ching (7d ama).
Asia/Oceania: Krit Jamkachornkiat (7d ama).
